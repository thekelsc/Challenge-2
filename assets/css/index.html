About

Kelsey Creveling has over a decade in management consulting and large scale organizational transformation across the Healthcare and Oil & Gas industries, recently serving as a Principal Director of Industry Innovation within the Health and Public Services organization for Accenture Health before launching her own ventures.
Ms. Creveling now owns and operates KelsC. Consulting – a consultancy focused on the continued improvement of both professional service firms and the development and growth of individuals. Under the KelsC. Consulting collection of ventures she recently launched The Consultant's Closet – a website focused on providing wardrobe guidance and attire inspiration for the professional's lifestyle.
In addition, Ms. Creveling is a founding Managing Partner with Säfik Advsrs – a tech startup focused consulting firm founded to support new venture sustainable growth.
